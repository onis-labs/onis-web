Privacy Policy

Your habits are private by design.

This Privacy Policy explains how ONIS handles your information when you use the ONIS iPhone and Apple Watch app. ONIS is built with a privacy-first approach: your habit data stays with you.

Effective date and updates

Effective date: May 21, 2026

Last updated: May 21, 2026

If this policy changes, we will update this page and revise the "Last updated" date.

1. Our privacy principles

  • We collect as little information as possible.
  • Your habit data is stored on your device and in your iCloud account for sync.
  • We do not sell personal data and do not run third-party ad trackers.

2. Information we collect

ONIS does not require account registration. The app stores habit content (such as habit names, completion history, streaks, and reminder settings) on your device.

If iCloud sync is enabled, this data syncs through Apple's CloudKit infrastructure linked to your Apple ID.

3. HealthKit data

ONIS may request optional access to Apple HealthKit data (for example workouts or mindful minutes) to help reduce duplicate habit logging.

  • Health data access is opt-in and controlled by you in iOS settings.
  • ONIS reads only the data types you authorize.
  • ONIS does not write to HealthKit unless explicitly shown in-app.
  • HealthKit data is not used for advertising or sold to third parties.

4. iCloud and CloudKit sync

When iCloud sync is active, your app data is stored and transmitted using Apple CloudKit in your iCloud account. We do not operate a separate habit-data server and do not have direct access to your private CloudKit records.

5. Analytics and diagnostics

ONIS does not include third-party behavioral ad SDKs. Basic anonymous diagnostics may be received through Apple's built-in crash and analytics tooling if you enable those settings with Apple.

6. Third-party services

ONIS does not share habit content with social networks, advertising networks, or external data brokers.

7. Children's privacy

ONIS is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your rights and choices

  • You can disable notifications and Health access in iOS settings at any time.
  • You can delete habit data directly in-app or by removing the app and related iCloud data.
  • You may contact us for privacy requests.

9. International users (GDPR and CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, delete, or object to processing of personal information. Because ONIS is designed to minimize data collection and keep habit records in your Apple environment, most control remains directly with you.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, email support@onisapp.com.
