FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you'd want to know before downloading.

Is ONIS free to download?

Yes. ONIS is completely free to download and use — no subscription, no paywalled features, and no hidden purchases.

Do I need an Apple Watch to use ONIS?

Apple Watch is where ONIS shines brightest, but the app works on iPhone alone too. You can track habits, view streaks, and see progress without a Watch — but the wrist-tap experience is what ONIS was built for.

Where is my data stored?

Your habit data lives on your device. If you have iCloud Drive enabled, ONIS syncs automatically through Apple's CloudKit — your data stays in your iCloud account, not on our servers. We never see or have access to it.

How do I delete all my data?

To delete all habit data: open ONIS → Settings → Reset All Data. To also remove iCloud sync data: iPhone Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage → ONIS → Delete Data. You can also delete the app entirely to remove all local data.

Does ONIS access Apple Health?

Only if you choose to allow it. ONIS can optionally read Health data — like steps, workouts, and mindful minutes — to reduce double-logging. This access is entirely opt-in, controlled in iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → ONIS. Health data is never used for advertising or shared with anyone.

What iOS and watchOS version do I need?

ONIS requires iOS 17 or later and, for Apple Watch features, watchOS 10 or later on your paired Apple Watch.
