FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you'd want to know before downloading.

Is ONIS free to download?

Yes. ONIS is completely free — no subscription, no paywalled features, and no hidden purchases.

Do I need an Apple Watch to use ONIS?

Apple Watch is where ONIS shines brightest, but the app works on iPhone alone too. You can track habits, view streaks, and see progress without a Watch.

Where is my data stored?

Your habit data lives on your device. If iCloud Drive is enabled, ONIS syncs through Apple's CloudKit — your data stays in your iCloud account, not on our servers.

How do I delete all my data?

Open ONIS → Settings → Reset All Data. For iCloud data: iPhone Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage → ONIS → Delete Data.

Does ONIS access Apple Health?

Only if you choose to allow it. Health access is entirely opt-in, controlled in iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → ONIS. Never used for advertising.

What iOS and watchOS version do I need?

ONIS requires iOS 17 or later and, for Apple Watch features, watchOS 10 or later.
