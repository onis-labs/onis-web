Frequently asked questions

Getting started

Is ONIS free to use?

Yes. ONIS is currently free with no subscription required.

Do I need to create an account?

No account is required. Your data stays on-device and optionally in your iCloud account for sync.

Can I use ONIS without Apple Watch?

Yes. ONIS works on iPhone, but Apple Watch gives the fastest one-tap logging experience.

Habits and streaks

How are streaks calculated?

A streak increases when you complete a habit on each required day based on its schedule.

Can I edit a missed day?

Yes. Open a habit's history view and adjust entries where the app permits manual corrections.

Can I pause a habit?

You can change frequency or archive a habit to temporarily stop streak pressure.

Apple Watch

Why do taps on Watch feel delayed?

Check Bluetooth and watch connectivity. Delays usually resolve once watchOS sync catches up.

How do I add ONIS as a complication?

Press and hold your watch face, tap Edit, choose a complication slot, and select ONIS.

Why is the Watch app missing?

Open the Watch app on iPhone, scroll to Available Apps, and install ONIS manually if needed.

Privacy and troubleshooting

Does ONIS track me or sell data?

No. ONIS is designed for private habit tracking and does not sell personal data.

What if my data is missing after reinstall?

Verify iCloud sync is enabled and you are signed into the same Apple ID used previously.

How do I fully delete my data?

You can clear data from app settings and remove ONIS data from iCloud storage settings if needed.

Troubleshooting

Watch complication not updating

  1. Confirm ONIS has notification/background permissions.
  2. Open ONIS on iPhone once to refresh sync state.
  3. Restart Apple Watch, then re-add the complication.

Streak reset unexpectedly

  1. Check habit schedule (daily vs selected days).
  2. Review timezone/date settings on iPhone and Watch.
  3. Use history view to confirm whether day completion was recorded.

Data missing after reinstall

  1. Sign into the same Apple ID as before.
  2. Ensure iCloud Drive is enabled for ONIS.
  3. Wait a few minutes for CloudKit sync to repopulate data.
